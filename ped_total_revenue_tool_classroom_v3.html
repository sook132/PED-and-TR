Diagram key:
Blue line = demand curve
Grey dashed lines = original price and quantity
Red dashed lines = new price and quantity after price increase
Grey shaded rectangle = original total revenue area
Red shaded rectangle = new total revenue area
Green shaded strip = revenue gained from higher price on units still sold
Orange shaded block = revenue lost from fewer units sold
When demand is price inelastic, a rise in price causes a less than proportionate fall in quantity demanded, so total revenue rises.

Step-by-step explanation
